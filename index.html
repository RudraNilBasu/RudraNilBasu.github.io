About Me

Hello! I am Rudra Nil Basu , I make games, and welcome to my portfolio.

Currently I am a second year undergraduate in Computer Science and Technology at West Bengal University Of Technology . I did my schooling at Vivekananda Mision School where I pursued with Science stream in the 10+2 level

I have been in video game development for the past 3 years, and have worked on games of various genres and platforms. I generally take care of the design and programming aspect of the games, though there are few games which I did on my own taking care of the visual aspects also. All of these games are showcased in this website. Currently, I use the Unity Game Engine

I have participated in various Game Jams (both International and National Competitions) and did fairly well in some of them, most notably being the Runner's up in Student Game Of the Year, organised by NASSCOM Gaming Forum and grabbing the third spot in GameJolt's 2015 Asylum Jam
