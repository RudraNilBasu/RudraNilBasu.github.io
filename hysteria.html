Hysteria

Overview

Title : Hysteria

Genre : Horror

Type : 3D

Made With : Unity

Platform : Windows PC

Current State : Work in Progress (Jam Version available)

Hysteria was originally made for the 2015 Asylum Jam where it took the third place overall. Since then, I was working on the game, polishing stuff. The jam version was made within 48 hours of time span, and the main idea was to control two different person two escape from an unknown dungeon.

Post-jam, I am completely re-doing the whole game, making it more polished, and also will be changing the main story too. This version of the game will be a prequel of a major horror game which I am working on, Apparition.
