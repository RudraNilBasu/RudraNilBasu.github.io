About Me

Currently I am pursuing B.Tech in Computer Science in West Bengal University of Technology in India.

I started making games since 2013 after coming across the Unity Game Engine and I have been making game since then. As a developer, I mostly concentrate on the programming aspect of the game, though there are certain games (mostly the games from the game jams) in which I worked on the art and the music. Currently, besides the programming part, I am also focusing more on the design aspect of games. All my games has been made on the Unity engine, and currently I am trying my hands on Unreal Engine.

Most of my recent games are released under the name of PriZm, with which I have won a few awards on various events. Currently I am working on an extended remake of Hysteria (which was originally made for a game jam).

I also play a lot of games, both indie and AAA titles, and I am specifically addicted to the horror genre.

Besides this, I am also into various other activities in my free time like competitive programming ( with the handle r_n_b), open source contribution, reading and answering on Quora and watch few genre-specific movies/TV shows in general.
